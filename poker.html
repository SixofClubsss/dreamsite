Private Poker Tables

﻿

The concept behind these poker tables is to give players control of their own private table without any third party. The owner of the table sets the ante/limits then invites players to join. This gives the game more of a personal feel than traditional online poker. Similar to our Baccarat we are using an app to interface with the table, the smart contract controls all the cards and betting. Winner takes the Dero pot. Poker tables are currently in the testing phase. The plan is to have different table types (hold'em, five card) and to make them customizable. Stayed tuned for more details on how to get your own table.
