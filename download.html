The Fun Doesn't Stop Here

Our smart contract design allows for the deck of cards to be used in making other playing card games. Many games outside of cards would be a great fit for this private table model as well. Although we are calling them private tables it is possible to use them in sit & go style applications, and we have used them to hold private tournaments.  Any currencies in the Dero network can be added to the tables. With NFA integration tables are fully customizable in many ways, stay tuned for updates. If you'd like help with a custom table feel free to contact us about your inquiry.
