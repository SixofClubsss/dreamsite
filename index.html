Open source crypto games built on Deros private smart contract platform

All that we see or seem is but a dReam within a dReam

-Egar Allan Poe

Many of the centralized gaming platforms the algorithms remain a mystery. You rely on unseen forces to determine the fairness and outcome of the game. dReam Tables removes the third party involvement and gives players their power back. All our games are open source, verifiable and operate in a decentralized manner. The apps run locally on your computer. Unlike most crypto games that use public ledgers, at dReam Tables your balances & transactions are stored on a private ledger allowing you to play in complete privacy. We use the power of Deros private smart contract platform to achieve this. With our private table model we aim to give digital games a bit more of a personal feel. Once you have a dReam Table you are in control, play as you please. 

This project is community funded and driven.
