Open source crypto games built on Dero's private smart contract platform

 You probably wouldn't play cards with a blindfold on, right? Well with many of the digital gaming platforms you are doing just that. The game algorithms often remain a mystery. You rely on unseen, third party forces to determine the fairness and outcome of the game. In some instances they can even refuse to payout a players winnings. With dReam Tables, we want to remove the third party from this equation. All our games are open source, verifiable and operate in a decentralized manner. The apps run locally on your computer. Unlike most crypto games that use public ledgers, at dReam Tables your balances & transactions are stored on a private ledger allowing you to play in complete privacy. We use the power of Dero's private smart contract platform to achieve this. With our private table model we aim to give digital games a bit more of a personal feel. Once you have a dReam Table you are in control, play as you please. 


﻿

About The Project

dReam Tables is a combination of a few passions. Cards, privacy coins and how the technology behind them can benefit the individual. Cryptocurrencies are becoming more and more prevalent in the world and that may or may not turn out to be a good thing. We believe that privacy coins like Dero will play a big role in making it a positive outcome for humanity. And what better way to showcase a privacy coins abilities than to build some kick ass games with it. The custom artwork is a big focus in the games, some of it years in the making. While we do have live tables set up like you would see at a casino, our main mission is not to be a casino ourselves. We want to empower players through our open source games, by removing third party control and giving it back to the players. Another issue we see with many digital card games is, they can feel like you are just pushing buttons on a screen without any real interaction. We want the games to feel more like you are at home playing with family or friends, the private table model suits this goal well. Some may not be as card crazy as we are, so as the project grows we would like to make some other house hold favourite games into dReam Tables. 

This project is community funded and driven.
