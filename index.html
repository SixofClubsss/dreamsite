Poker

Open source crypto games built on Dero's private smart contract platform

With many of the centralized gaming platforms, the algorithms remain a mystery. You rely on unseen forces to determine the fairness and outcome of the game. dReam Tables removes the third party involvement and gives players their power back. All our games are open source, verifiable and operate in a decentralized manner. The apps run locally on your computer. Unlike most crypto games that use public ledgers, at dReam Tables your balances & transactions are stored on a private ledger allowing you to play in complete privacy. We use the power of Dero's private smart contract platform to achieve this. With our private table model we aim to give digital games a bit more of a personal feel. Once you have a dReam Table you are in control, play as you please. 

This project is community funded and driven. 

Visit the Contact page for more info on how you can help contribute.

dReams Token

dReams is our fixed supply token used at dReam Tables. Built on Dero, dReam balances remain fully private. It has a max supply of 1 Million and is redeemable for Dero at a constant exchange rate of 1:333 dReams.
